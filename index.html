About Us


happier is a web based app where you will register in, fill in your current life style details like how many hours do you sleep daily, what kind of food do you usually eat, whether you are dealing with any kind of mental struggles like anxiety or depression and the amount of daily physical activity that you do. Next it will start to suggest a customized plan for you including daily activities that should make you overall healthier depending on your currnet life style.
